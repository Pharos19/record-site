Services

Standard Items - The Essentials

Ceremony Music & Sound

DJ to provide mic for officiant, readers, musicians as necessary; will run sound and start music thirty minutes prior to ceremony start time, as your guests are being seated. After ceremony, DJ transitions to cocktail music and is available for announcements, etc.

Cocktail Music

Music for you and your guests to enjoy before your dinner begins. DJ will be available to make any necessary announcements during this time.

Dinner Music

You and your guests will be tapping their toes and moving in their seats because your DJ knows how to build the energy in the room all the way to the dance floor! DJ will facilitate introductions, speeches, and any other special happenings that you decide upon ahead of time, then seamlessly shift into dance mode to keep the party going all night long.

Classic Package - Four Hours of Dancing

When the dance portion of your party begins, Record Entertainment leads the way - providing ALL THE MUSIC and ALL THE FUN! Professionally trained DJ kicks off your grand march, transitions into specialty dances, and reads the room to keep you and your guests on the dance floor. Everyone will be talking about the fun they had at your wedding for years to come! Pre-wedding consultation, travel, standard light show, and setup included.

Auxiliary Items - Spice Up Your Party!

Photo Booth

3 hours of our photo booth complete with props, unlimited photos for your guests, an attendant to operate the booth, and a book of all of the photos put together for the wedding couple. Travel & setup included.

Uplights (4)

Colorful accent lighting to highlight areas at your reception. Color(s), placement, and number of lights can be determined closer to the wedding.

Monogram Light

A beautiful, custom designed monogram with your names, initials, and date; projected where you choose - above your head table, your dance floor, you name it!

MultiMedia Memories

Video creation of 3 vignettes of your photos: 25 + 25 photos of each partner individually, and 25 photos of the couple together - edited to music selections of your choice and displayed on our projector and screen at your reception.

Additional Services

Karaoke

Club DJ

Take your club experience to new heights with our dynamic DJ services, boasting a proven track record of success and seamless transitions. Whether its for special events or a weekend night of non-stop dancing, trust us to elevate your Club DJ needs.

Grad Party DJ Services

Age appropriate DJ and music suitable for your party; playing radio-edited music without offensive lyrics. Interactive DJ that knows what to play and when to play it to build energy at your event = All The Music, All The Fun!

School Dances

Experience age-appropriate, professionally curated DJ services tailored for students and administrators, featuring radio-edited music free from offensive lyrics. Our interactive DJs possess expert knowledge of music selection and timing, effortlessly building energy on the dancefloor while adding games, interaction, etc = All The Music, All The Fun! Lesser hours needed will bring down the price.

Super DJ Lighting

Indulge in an exhilarating visual spectacle with our overhead light truss, brimming with an array of pulsating lights that ignite the dance floor with excitement! Our expert team seamlessly integrates the truss into our existing dynamic light show kit, meticulously designed to elevate your event and create an immersive party atmosphere that will leave your guests mesmerized.

Additional Hour(s)

Experience non-stop dancing and uninterrupted entertainment as we cater to your guests' rhythm with options to extend your booking times, ensuring your party's timeline perfectly aligns with an unforgettable night of celebration.
